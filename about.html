THE NEW SEASON(CHAPTER 2 REMIX)

Fortnite’s Chapter 2 Remix has officially launched, delivering a month-long experience that blends nostalgia with music-inspired features. This unique season celebrates the Chapter 2 map and early gameplay while adding special points of interest (POIs) crafted in collaboration with famous artists like Snoop Dogg, Eminem, Ice Spice, and Juice WRLD. Each week, a different artist’s creative vision comes to life, bringing themed locations as well as skins, and exclusive items to Fortnite’s battlefield.

Here’s a breakdown of what players can expect throughout the Fortnite Chapter 2 Remix. From artist-inspired POIs and themed weapons to the new Remix Pass and the season’s major live events.

Chapter 2 Remix launched with three key dates:

  • November 1: Prelude to the Remix Season, introducing teasers and the first artist collaborations.
  • November 2: Official start of Chapter 2 Remix with the return of the Chapter 2 map.
  • November 30: The grand Remix Finale, a live event that concludes this unique season.Players can dive into revamped locations and weekly artist-focused updates, in addition to exclusive items and skins available throughout the month.

The Remix Pass and Skins

The Remix Pass, a short 70-tier battle pass, offers five exclusive remixed skins inspired by characters from the original Chapter 2 season. These skins include:
  • Chaos Director
  • 1-Ball
  • Undercover Skye
  • Meowdas
  • Dynamo TNTina

Players can unlock these skins and other cosmetics by progressing through the pass, which costs 950 V-Bucks.

In addition to these skins, each week brings new item shop cosmetics, including themed outfits for each collaborating artist.

New Shoe Cosmetics: Kicks

Chapter 2 Remix introduces Fortnite’s first “Kicks,” a pair of red and black Air Jordans available with over 95% of Fortnite’s skins. Epic Games plans to expand shoe compatibility across more skins by Spring 2025, allowing even more customisation options.

Weekly Artist Highlights

Each week of the season highlights a different musical artist, with unique POIs, themed weapons, and skins.
  • Week 1: Snoop Dogg

Kicking off the season, Snoop Dogg brings his signature style to Fortnite with “The Doggpound,” a reimagining of the original Agency POI. Players can unlock Snoop’s Drum Gun Mythic and access the LBC ‘93 Dogg Outfit via the Music Pass. His son, Cordell Broadus, describes the Doggpound as inspired by Snoop’s 30-room Los Angeles compound, featuring a casino, club, and basketball court.
  • Week 2: Eminem

Launching on November 7, Eminem’s “Spaghetti Grotto” is a re-envisioned Grotto POI, a nod to his Detroit restaurant, Mom’s Spaghetti. This week features the RG Minigun, dubbed the “Rap God” machine gun, firing lyrics from Eminem’s hit song, “Rap God.” The “Rap Boy Reloaded” skin is also available in the item shop.
  • Week 3: Ice Spice

On November 14, Ice Spice’s presence transforms Shark Island into “Ice Isle,” a Y2K-inspired, neon-lit shopping mall, reminiscent of her New York roots. Her signature weaponry includes the Ice Spice Grappler and Ice Spice Mythic Rifle, both found on the island. Her Rap Princess and Ice Spice outfits feature jewellery by Benny The Jeweler, adding extra sparkle to her appearance.
  • Week 4: Juice WRLD

Juice WRLD’s legacy is honoured in the final week starting November 21. The WRLD Point landmark appears, along with the Slayer Juice WRLD Outfit, a cel-shaded tribute to the artist. Fortnite worked closely with his estate to design this memorial, and his mother, Carmela Wallace, emphasised Juice WRLD’s connection to Fortnite as a social platform.

Finale Event and Free Skin Giveaway

To celebrate the season’s conclusion, Fortnite will host the Remix Finale live event on November 30. During this finale, players who log in between 1 AM ET (6 AM GMT) on November 30 and 1 AM ET on December 1 will receive the Slayer Juice WRLD Outfit for free, honouring his passion for gaming and his love for Fortnite.

New Fortnite Festival and Music Pass

In addition to the battle royale map changes, Snoop Dogg headlines Fortnite Festival Season 6 with his cel-shaded skin and accompanying Music Pass. This pass is priced at 1,400 V-Bucks and features:
  • Tracks by artists like Juice WRLD, Big Sean, Soulja Boy, and Snoop Dogg’s “Young, Wild, and Free” with Wiz Khalifa and Bruno Mars.
  • Festival instruments and unique cosmetics available through the Music Pass. The Fortnite Festival will continue until January 14, providing fans with an extended musical experience beyond the Remix Finale.

Downtime and Server Maintenance

Epic Games has scheduled extended server maintenance on November 2. Downtime beginning at 6 AM GMT and expected to last approximately seven hours. The game’s update, v32.00, will require a larger download than previous patches, and players are advised to download and install the update before logging back into the game.

Fortnite Chapter 2 Remix provides an immersive experience for both new and nostalgic players, bridging the world of music with Fortnite’s beloved battle royale gameplay. Be sure to join in, explore the musical POIs, and celebrate the season’s grand finale on November 30!
