The best items(non-weapons)

Med Kit

Med Kits are good to carry, especially if you have a medallion.

They are useful if you're taking storm damage or were stuck in a storm.

Rare but not too rare, can carry up to three, refills your entire health.

Definitely something you always want to carry with you if you can.

Chug Jug

After a fight with a lot of damage taken, you can easily pop a Chug Jug and get full health and shield back.

The problem with this item is that you can't often use it while being contested.

The only mistake people make is using a Chug Jug for very little health and wasting it.

It's super rare and takes forever to drink, but it is the the second or first best healing item.

Mini Shield Potion

The fact that you can carry around six of them and they take less time to drink makes these overall better than the big shield sometimes.

Although they only go up to 50, It's the best and quickest way to gain shield when you're fighting an opponent

Big Shield Potion

Essential for fighting a sweat. To have a good chance of winning a fight you need full health.

Comes in three, gives the same amount of shield as minis(50 each) so minis and Big Pots pair well.

Slurp Juice

Slurp Juices are a perfect way to gain back everything while you're in battle, and you can usually use a Slurp Juice in time.

It takes three seconds, while the Chug Jug take 15 seconds, so in my opinion it is the best healing item.

Grappler

Grapplers are effective for many reasons, like rotations and getting away from players.

You can use them to gain high ground or get on top of buildings or builds.

Shockwave Grenade

Sends you flying when you throw it at the ground. Comes in six and is good for getting out of a fight.

Jetpack

The newest jetpack you can boost on and off which can make you go super far within a few seconds.

Boogie Bomb

Boogies are a cool item not necessary but good for funny clips.

Launch Pad

Good for rotations and to get out of storm.
